We designed an antimicrobial peptide KLWKKIKKLAKKV targeting E.coli.
The peptide adopts an amphipathic α-helical structure with a net charge of +5.
MIC against E.coli was 12.5 μM. The mechanism involves membrane disruption
through pore formation in the bacterial membrane.

Another sequence RRWRIVVIRK showed excellent activity against S.aureus with
MIC of 8.2 μM. This cationic peptide contains high lysine and arginine content.
